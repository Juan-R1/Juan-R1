```text
Juan, this application needs your input: [specific issue]. Role: [job title] at [company]. Link: [URL]. Digital Juan recommends: [approve / skip / provide info / review terms]. Reason: [short reason].
```

```text

I prepared the resume, answers, and application packet. Find it here: [packet path]. Recommended action: open the application and complete the verification manually.
```
