# Digital Juan Job Scoring Rubric

Score each role out of 100. Use short evidence notes. Do not generate a full packet for jobs below 75 unless Juan explicitly asks.

| Category | Points | What To Reward |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Pay potential | 20 | $70k+ is strong, $60k-$70k target, $50k-$60k acceptable bridge, unknown salary needs research, below $50k usually skip. |
| Healthcare/behavioral health fit | 20 | Healthcare, behavioral health, payer, hospital, population health, quality, care coordination, public health, clinical research. |
| Analytics/project/reporting fit | 15 | SQL, Python, Excel, Power BI, Tableau, reporting, dashboards, data cleaning, KPIs, workflow analysis, research data. |
| Remote/hybrid/location fit | 15 | Remote first, hybrid El Paso second, on-site El Paso third, Texas hybrid acceptable, relocation only for strong pay and growth. |
| Career growth/launch potential | 15 | Clear bridge to healthcare analytics, operations, QI, clinical research, program leadership, or $70k+ path. |
| Requirements realism | 10 | Matches Juan's background without false claims; no RN/LPC/LCSW/Epic/coding cert if required. |
| Portfolio alignment | 5 | Can mention healthcare-readmission-risk, cost-of-living, Divvy, social dashboard, or profile README credibly. |

## Decision Thresholds

| Score | Decision | Action |
| ---: | --- | --- |
| 85-100 | Apply now | Tailor strongly and prepare packet. |
| 75-84 | Apply if salary/company is good | Generate concise packet. |
| 65-74 | Maybe | Apply only if low effort, strategic, or salary is compelling. |
| 50-64 | Usually skip | Save one-line reason only. |
| Below 50 | Skip | Do not spend tailoring tokens. |

## Labels

- Launch Role: strong fit, strong growth, likely useful for next level.
- Strong Bridge Role: not perfect, but moves Juan toward healthcare/data/operations.
- Safe Pay Bump: better compensation with realistic requirements.
- Stretch Worth Trying: ambitious but plausible without false claims.
- Low-Value Role: possible but weak upside.
- Skip: unsafe, underpaid, predatory, unrealistic, or credential mismatch.

## Heavy Penalties

- RN, LPC, LCSW, licensed clinician, therapist, medical provider, or Epic certification required.
- Medical coding certification required unless entry-level training is provided.
- Senior data scientist, machine learning engineer, software engineer, or senior BI architect positioning.
- 5+ years direct analytics required for a purely analytics role.
- Commission-only, MLM language, unpaid internship, vague company, upfront payment, or suspicious personal-email application.
- Relocation required below $60k.
- Salary below $50k with no strategic upside.
