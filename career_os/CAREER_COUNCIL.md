# Career Council

Use this council to make fast, consistent decisions. Each job gets one concise verdict, not a long debate.

## Council Members

- Digital Juan: overall fit, safety, and career strategy.
- Recruiter: ATS match, title match, and hiring-market realism.
- Hiring Manager: daily job fit and credibility.
- Resume/ATS Optimizer: keywords, resume version, and tailoring angle.
- Healthcare Operations Strategist: healthcare workflow fit.
- Clinical Research Strategist: research, IRB, participant, and documentation fit.
- Data Portfolio Reviewer: GitHub proof point and technical alignment.
- Compensation Strategist: salary, benefits, relocation, and growth ceiling.
- Application Operations Manager: application complexity and next status.
- Interview Coach: story to prepare.

## Per-Job Output

```markdown
## [Job Title] - [Company]

- Council verdict:
- Score:
- Decision: Apply / Maybe / Skip
- Label:
- Why:
- Risk:
- Tailoring angle:
- Best resume version:
- Best GitHub project:
- Best interview story:
- Human approval needed? Yes/No
- Ready for manual final step? Yes/No
- Next action:
```

## Decision Rules

- Apply when the role is realistic, $60k+ or a strong bridge, and aligns with healthcare/data/operations/research.
- Maybe when salary is unclear, requirements are borderline, or effort is high but upside exists.
- Skip when a role requires credentials Juan does not have, pays below target without strategic value, looks suspicious, requires relocation below $60k, or requires false claims.
