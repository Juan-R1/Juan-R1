# Next-Level Role Map

## A. Fastest Interview Probability

- Role titles: Program Coordinator, Behavioral Health Program Specialist, Care Coordination Analyst, Quality Coordinator, Research Coordinator, Case Management Lead.
- Boolean terms: `(program coordinator OR behavioral health specialist OR quality coordinator OR research coordinator) AND (healthcare OR behavioral health OR community health)`.
- Companies/industries: EHN upward roles, UMC El Paso, Texas Tech Health El Paso, FQHCs, public health, behavioral health nonprofits.
- Why it fits: Closest to current experience and bilingual coordination background.
- Main risk: Pay may be below target.
- How to tailor: Lead with caseload, resource linkage, documentation, and multidisciplinary coordination.

## B. Best Pay Jump

- Role titles: Healthcare Business Analyst, Healthcare Data Analyst, Reporting Analyst Healthcare, Population Health Analyst, Healthcare Operations Analyst.
- Boolean terms: `("healthcare business analyst" OR "healthcare data analyst" OR "population health analyst" OR "reporting analyst") AND (remote OR Texas)`.
- Companies/industries: Optum, CVS/Aetna, Centene, Elevance, Humana, Cigna/Evernorth, Molina, BCBS plans.
- Why it fits: Payers and large healthcare companies often pay in the $60k-$70k+ range.
- Main risk: Direct analytics experience requirements.
- How to tailor: Bridge healthcare workflow knowledge with SQL/Python/Excel/BI portfolio.

## C. Best Long-Term Career Path

- Role titles: Healthcare Operations Analyst, Quality Improvement Analyst, Population Health Analyst, Clinical Data Coordinator, Business Analyst Healthcare.
- Boolean terms: `(quality improvement OR healthcare operations OR population health OR clinical data OR business analyst) AND (analytics OR reporting OR dashboard OR KPI)`.
- Companies/industries: Payers, hospital systems, public health, health tech, CROs.
- Why it fits: Builds toward healthcare analytics/operations leadership.
- Main risk: May need stronger BI artifacts or formal analyst experience.
- How to tailor: Emphasize service workflow insight, documentation quality, and reporting mindset.

## D. Best Remote Fit

- Role titles: Utilization Management Coordinator, Healthcare Data Coordinator, Clinical Data Coordinator, Reporting Analyst, Program Coordinator Remote.
- Boolean terms: `(remote) AND ("clinical data coordinator" OR "utilization management coordinator" OR "healthcare data coordinator" OR "reporting analyst")`.
- Companies/industries: Payers, CROs, remote-first healthcare vendors, FlexJobs/Remote.co/Built In sources.
- Why it fits: Remote work broadens market beyond El Paso.
- Main risk: Competition and credential filters.
- How to tailor: Lead with bilingual communication, documentation reliability, and tool stack.

## E. Best Use Of Current Healthcare Experience

- Role titles: Behavioral Health Program Specialist, Quality Coordinator, Care Coordination Analyst, Population Health Coordinator, Community Programs Specialist.
- Boolean terms: `("behavioral health" OR "care coordination" OR "community health" OR "population health") AND (coordinator OR analyst OR specialist)`.
- Companies/industries: Behavioral health, public health, FQHCs, hospitals, Medicaid/payer organizations.
- Why it fits: Current experience is a differentiator.
- Main risk: Some roles may be lateral or underpaid.
- How to tailor: Emphasize EHN work, LOC familiarity, care transitions, and resource navigation.

## F. Best Use Of GitHub Portfolio

- Role titles: Healthcare Data Analyst, Reporting Analyst, General Data Analyst, Business Analyst, Operations Analyst.
- Boolean terms: `(data analyst OR reporting analyst OR operations analyst OR business analyst) AND (SQL OR Python OR Excel OR Power BI OR Tableau)`.
- Companies/industries: Healthcare analytics, payer operations, operations analytics, public sector, consulting/staffing.
- Why it fits: Projects can prove analyst readiness.
- Main risk: Portfolio may need interview-ready walkthroughs.
- How to tailor: Mention healthcare-readmission-risk first for healthcare roles, then Divvy or cost-of-living depending on the posting.

## G. Too Much Stretch Right Now

- Role titles: Senior Data Scientist, Machine Learning Engineer, Software Engineer, Senior BI Architect, Epic Analyst requiring certification, RN/LPC/LCSW roles.
- Boolean terms: Do not prioritize.
- Companies/industries: Any.
- Why it does not fit: Requires credentials, seniority, or experience Juan should not claim.
- Main risk: Wastes time and weakens positioning.
- How to tailor: Do not tailor unless requirements are clearly entry-level and truthful.
